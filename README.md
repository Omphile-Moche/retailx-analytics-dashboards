## Retail Analytics & Business Intelligence Solution
### Project Overview
This project involved building an end-to-end data analytics and business intelligence solution for a simulated South African retail organization. The goal was to integrate, transform, model, and analyze datasets to support decision-making related to sales performance, customer insights, and regional growth.
### Tools Used
• Power BI <br>
• R <br>
• DAX <br>
### Key Tasks Performed
•Developed interactive Power BI multi-page report to analyze product performance, customer behavior, and regional trends <br>
• Designed a star schema data model <br>
• Developed DAX measures for revenue, profit, and growth metrics <br>
• Performed data cleaning and transformation <br>
### Key Insights
• Approximately 65% year-over-year revenue growth, 41.51% profit margin <br>
• Identified top-performing regions and product categories <br>
